Spotify Playlist Generator

Web App | React & Node.js | Winter 2020

Tired of listening to the same songs? This playlist generator creates a brand new playlist of 20 songs recommended just for you based on the songs in one of your current playlists. Login with your Spotify account to allow the application to access your library. Then just type in your Spotify username, the name of the playlist you want your recommendations based on, and the name of your new playlist! The details of the recommended songs show up at the bottom of the page, and the new playlist is automatically saved to your Spotify library.

Song recommendations are generated based on randomly selected seed tracks from the chosen playlist and the average audio features of songs in the playlist, e.g. danceability, acousticness, loudness, speechiness, energy, etc. The application also supports form validation and error handling for calls to the Spotify Web API. Authorization is supported by React Spotify Auth.
