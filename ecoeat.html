EcoEat

Mobile App | React Native & Firebase | Summer 2020

What happens to the food we don't eat? Leftovers get dumped, and the little scraps of food you can't finish are tossed in the trash. It seems like just a little here and there, no big deal. But every year, US households waste a total of almost 80 billion pounds of food. This is approximately 30-40% of the entire US food supply, and most of the wasted food ends up in landfills around the country. Crazy right?

EcoEat enables you to reduce food waste while also getting custom recipe recommendations. Simply add the ingredients you have on hand to your virtual pantry and instantly get recommendations based on those ingredients. When you find a recipe you love, you can bookmark it to easily find it again. EcoEat helps you save time, money, and our planet.

Some recipes have a green leaf tag indicating that the recipe is a vegan or vegetarian option. The meat industry is a major contributor to the production of greenhouse gases, and a more plant-based diet significantly reduces your carbon footprint. Together, we can all do our part in working torwards a more sustainable tomorrow.
