EZSave

Web App | React & Node.js | May 2020

Budgeting is an often overlooked but extremely necessary thing to do. Whether you want to save up for a new car, limit your late night food runs, or simply see how much you're spending, EZSave is the app for you.

Just set a monthly budget, and input your expenses. Classify them with categories such as bills, entertainment, food, transport, etc to easily see where most of your money goes. EZSave then presents you with a dashboard of analytics showing data such as a graph of your monthly spending trends, how much you've spent this month, and how much of your monthly budget you have left.

Chances are, you're spending more than you think. With just a swipe of a card or click of a button, those shoes you've been eyeing are now yours. But budgeting doesn't have to be hard. Use EZSave — personal finance made easy.
