Alien Invaders

Python

Date: May 2019


This game is a take on the classic arcade game, Space Invaders, that uses stateful controllers to run an interactive application. The player gets 3 lives, and in order to win, the player must shoot all the aliens. If the aliens cross the defense line or the player loses all 3 lives, then the aliens win.

Upon initializing the application, the player can choose to how many aliens to battle and what speed at which the aliens will move. The game incorporates programming animation, collisions, and sound to enhance user experience. A sound plays every time an alien fires or the player fires as well as when an alien bolt collides with the player's ship or the player's bolt hits an alien. The sound can be turned on and off during the game by pressing 'h' and 'f', respectively.

If the player destroyes all the aliens in the wave without losing a life, a new wave of aliens is created that moves 0.8 times faster than the previous wave. The score is updated live as the player destroys aliens. 30 points are awarded if the player destroys an alien in the front row of its column, and 15 points if the alien is behind another.
