WAY Contactless Payment

Mobile App | Android Native & Java | Summer 2020

There's no doubt that COVID-19 has fundamentally changed the way restaurants do business. Online ordering and curbside pickup are now the norm, and that doesn't seem to be changing anytime soon.

WAY is a contactless payment system improving the ordering experience for both local restaurants and customers in Ithaca, NY. Simply scan a QR code to choose one of your favorite restaurants in Collegetown, and place an order in the mobile app or through the online web application. You'll be notified when your order is ready for pickup. No need to wait in line or worry about the potential health risks of ordering in-store. It doesn't get easier than that.

On the restaurant side, merchants can see data analytics, helping them grow their businesses and boost sales without a hefty commission. They are notified of incoming orders through their own app, and your food is then prepared and packaged for pickup. All payment processing is contact-free: no pens, no cash, no credit cards, no paper receipts. So choose WAY — the new way forward.
