Hello, I'm Sam.

I'm a sophomore at Cornell University studying computer science. Although I didn't grow up with a background in programming, I found myself in a computing class in my first year in college and quickly grew to love it.

A lot has changed since then. I switched majors, one of the riskiest yet best decisions I've ever made. Since I was young, I've always loved design, and computing is the intersection of design and technology. There is so much still yet to be done in this world. This is a field in which technology is being used to change lives, and I want to be a part of that.
